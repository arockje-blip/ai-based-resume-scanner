# AI Resume Scanner
## Industrial Implementation & Impact

---

## Why Industrial Adoption Now

- Recruiting volume is rising while HR teams are flat
- ATS filters are more rigid; good candidates are lost
- Talent markets move fast; time-to-hire is critical
- AI can standardize resume quality at scale

---

## Industrial Use Cases

- Enterprises: high-volume hiring pipelines
- Staffing firms: faster candidate screening
- Universities: career center services
- Government/PSUs: standardized, fair screening

---

## Implementation Overview

**Core Workflow**
1. Candidate submits resume
2. Job description selected
3. AI analysis + match score generated
4. Results delivered to candidate + recruiter

**Deployment Options**
- On-premises for privacy
- Private cloud for scale
- Hybrid for compliance

---

## Technical Integration

- API-first architecture (easy ATS/HRIS integration)
- SSO + role-based access for recruiters
- Logs and audit trails for compliance
- Secure storage (optional) with retention policy

---

## Data Privacy & Security

- No resume storage by default
- Optional encrypted storage for audits
- Configurable data retention windows
- GDPR-ready workflows and consent

---

## Rollout Plan (90 Days)

**Phase 1: Pilot (Weeks 1-3)**
- 1-2 departments
- Validate match accuracy
- HR feedback loop

**Phase 2: Expand (Weeks 4-8)**
- Add more job families
- Integrate with ATS
- Train HR teams

**Phase 3: Scale (Weeks 9-12)**
- Company-wide rollout
- KPI reporting dashboards
- Continuous model tuning
