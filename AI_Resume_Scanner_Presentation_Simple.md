# AI Resume Scanner
## Simple Pitch Deck

---

## Problem

- 75% of resumes are filtered before a human sees them
- ATS tools often reject good candidates
- Manual resume tailoring is slow and repetitive
- Job seekers miss roles due to keyword gaps

---

## Solution

- Upload resume (PDF, TXT, DOCX)
- Paste target job description
- Get AI analysis in about 30 seconds

**Output:**
- Match score (0-100)
- Missing keywords
- Improvement suggestions
- Rewritten professional summary

---

## How It Works

1. Upload resume
2. Paste job description
3. Click analyze
4. Review recommendations
5. Update resume and re-scan

---

## Why It Is Better

- Uses AI reasoning, not only exact keyword matching
- Understands context and synonyms
- Gives role-specific suggestions
- Privacy-first with self-hosted option
- Lower price than major competitors
