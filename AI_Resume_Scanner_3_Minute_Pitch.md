# AI Resume Scanner
## 3-Minute Overview

---

## The Problem

- 75% of resumes never reach a recruiter
- ATS filters reject qualified candidates
- Manual tailoring is slow and repetitive

---

## The Solution

- Upload resume + paste job description
- AI scores match (0-100)
- Highlights missing keywords
- Suggests improvements + new summary

---

## Why It Matters

- Faster hiring cycles
- Better fit between candidates and roles
- Lower cost per hire
- Improved candidate experience

1. Resume upload

3. AI analysis in ~30 seconds
4. Clear, actionable report
